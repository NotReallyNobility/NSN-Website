The Spirit World

The soul's afterlife is affected by what pantheon they follow. Yet what of those that follow no true pantheon? Well, ghosts have to exist somehow. The spirit world is somewhat loosely defined in structure, a hodgepodge of souls that had no place designated rest. The Council of The Conscious is the only true order in there, a collection of souls that managed to retain themselves, and does what they can to prevent other souls from losing their minds to the chaos.
