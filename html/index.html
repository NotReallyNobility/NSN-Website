Characters

If the wide screen didn't give it away, there are quite a few characters within my stories. Ranging from gods to demons, to gods that used to be demons, to mortals that suffer all because of one jerk who decided he wanted to be a god, NSN has quite the cast. Many of these characters have undergone years of stories and thus have quite the long pages. There are also sever groups, which can get their own groups. These stories often take advantage of different mythologies, adapting them in various ways and so pages will be dedicated to them as well, such as God, Krampus, and Zues.

About

NSN is an online psuedonym standing for "Not So Noble," a pun involving my last name. This website serves to catalog my plots, settings, and characters for myself and those close to me while I prepare to bring these stories to life. It should work similarly to an encyclopedia for my lore.
